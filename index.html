What Is Pin-Up

Starting with the Gibson Girls, pin-up photos are a symbol of sexuality. A Pin-up is a model whose mass-produced pictures see wide appeal as popular culture. These photos where originally aimed for informal display, to be ‘pinned-up’ everywhere and anywhere. Traditionally female models that posed in this style where called cheesecake models and male models beefcake models. Today pin-up photos are a symbol of body positivity, sexuality and acceptance of ones self.
